Don't compromise on security
Set up two factor authentication

We strongly suggest you to set up two factor authentication, this will make your wallet much more secure and will let you some additional features such as Spending Limits and Delayed Payments.

If you set it up to issue a transaction you will not only need to login to your wallet but also confirm it with a code sent to you via email, SMS or google authenticator as per your choice.
